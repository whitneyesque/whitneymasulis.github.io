    Export for printing

    Printing tips for Canon Inkjet Light-Fabric Transfer Paper
    • Use your printer's photo / high-quality setting, paper type = Plain Paper.
    • Feed the transfer sheet coated-side-down per the package instructions.
    • Let the print dry 1–2 minutes before peeling the backing for ironing.
    • Mirroring is required for light-fabric paper so the text reads correctly after transfer.
